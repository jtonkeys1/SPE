FAQs

How does SavePlanetEarth plan to combat the climate crisis?

With strategic coordination via nation-states such as Sri Lanka, Maldives, UK, & others worldwide, alongside using donations to indefinitely to fund the efforts, we plan to launch a massive initiative to help with carbon sequestration.


How does the sponsorship program and fund mechanism of SavePlanetEarth work?

SavePlanetEarth derives funds for growing trees through two main mechanisms: Climate wallet & direct sponsorships from donors, well-wishers, institutions, etc. The second mechanism of generating funds for SavePlanetEarth is through direct sponsorships from donors, well-wishers, institutions etc., that you can consider helping us be able to do!


What are Legal Safeguards for funds we help create?

All funds received and used by the SavePlanetEarth program is accountable for on the blockchain. As the program is registered also in the UK, it is subject to purview of and oversight by the British Accounting System. Our association with the Governments also keeps an austere, checks and balances system well in place.


If I were to contact SavePlanetEarth to form an Action Group in my own county, how do I start?

Contact the SavePlanetEarth team to learn more about starting a local Action Group!
